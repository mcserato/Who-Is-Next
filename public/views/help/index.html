Help Center

  • LOG-IN
    To gain access to the application, follow the steps specified below.
    Step 1: Enter a valid username.
    Step 2: Enter a valid password.
    Step 3: Click login button.
  • LOG-OUT
    To disconnect its access and services to the application, follow the steps specified below.
    Step 1: Click logout icon.
  • VIEW
    VIEW CLASS
    View all available classes.
    Step 1: Enter a valid username.

    VIEW STUDENT
    View all enlisted students.
    Step 1: Click Student button on the side bar.

    VIEW LOGS
    View all logs made by all users.
    Step 1: Click Logs button side bar.
  • SEARCH
    SEARCH STUDENT
    Search a specific student from all classes.
    Step 1: Enter either student's name or student number.

    Step 2: Confirm search.
    SEARCH STUDENT IN CLASS
    View all enlisted students.
    Step 1: Enter either student's name or student number.

    Step 2: Enter a class.
    Step 3: Confirm Search.
  • IMPORT STUDENT
    To import specific students from a section to another section, follow the steps below. Search a specific student from all classes.
    Step 1: Click on the Classes button and find the student to be imported.

    Step 2: On the list of commands on the student, click Import student.
    Step 3: Confirm the action of importing the students.

    SEARCH STUDENT IN CLASS
    To import specific students from the archived classes to another section, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click on the Archived Classes button and find the student to be imported.

    Step 2: On the list of commands on the student, click Import student.
    Step 3: Confirm the action of importing the student.
  • UPLOAD CSV FILE
    To upload a CSV file containing a list of students to be added, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click on the Classes button and search to the section you want to upload the students.
    Step 2: Click Import button and find the CSV file that you what to upload.
    Step 3: Wait for the confirmation of complete file loading.
  • VIEW ANALYTICS
    To view statistical data about the classes, sections, and students, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click on the View Analytics button.
  • SWITCH THEME
    To switch from one theme to another, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click on the Switch Theme button.
    Step 2: Choose from the list of available themes for the application.
    Step 3: Confirm the action of changing the application theme.
  • GET VOLUNTEERS
    Select One Volunteer
    To select one volunteer from the classes in the list, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click on the Get Volunteers button in the siebar.
    Step 2: Choose the option Select One Volunteer.
    Step 3: Choose the classes to be used in selecting volunteers .
    Step 4: Click the Get Volunteers button.

    To select one volunteer from each section from a specific class, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click Student button on the side bar.
    Step 2: Choose the option Select One Volunteer.
    Step 3: Choose the classes to be used in selecting volunteers .
    Step 4: Click the Get Volunteers button.

    Select N Volunteers
    To select N volunteers from the classes in the list, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: Click on the Get Volunteers button.
    Step 2: Choose the option Select N Volunteers.
    Step 3: Choose the classes to be used in selecting volunteers .
    Step 4: Click the Get Volunteers button.

    Mark/Unmark
    To mark or unmark students or sections before getting the list of volunteers, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: On the list of students to be chosen as volunteers, check or uncheck members.
    Step 2: Confirm the removal of the student from the list of volunteers.

    Remove Volunteer
    To remove students from the list of randomly generated volunteers, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: On the list of randomly generated the volunteers, click Remove on the students.
    Step 2: Confirm the removal of the volunteer from list.

    Set Frequency
    To set the maximum number of times a student can be chosen, follow the steps below.
    Step 1: While getting the number of volunteers, set the Max Frequency for selecting a student.

    Save Volunteers
    To save the list of randomly chosen volunteers, follow the steps below.
    Step 1:After viewing the list of randomly chosen volunteers, click Save Volunteers.
